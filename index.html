Column Basics

12 divs - 100% row coverage in large viewport

Twelve columns covered by twelve <div> in fluid display. At medium breakpoints (8-column support), 12 <div> will wrap to the next line. In the example, we add offset-1 on the medium breakpoint to the 1st and 7th elements to preserve symmetry on the medium-sized viewport.

8 divs - 100% row coverage in medium viewport

Eight columns covered by eight <div> in fluid display. At small breakpoints (4-column support), 8 <div> will wrap to the next line. Here, no offset is needed because the grid provides natural symmetry for the transition between 4- and 8-columns.
